## About Me

I'm a **software engineer and entrepreneur** based in the United States, studying at the **University of South Florida** while building products that solve real problems in the insurance industry.

I founded **[Caspian Solutions](https://caspiansol.com)**, where we're developing **Apex CRM** — a universal CRM platform purpose-built for Health and Life Insurance Agents. Apex CRM unifies client management, policy tracking, renewal pipelines, and agent performance in one modern platform.
